The flow fitting type IfcJunctionBoxType defines commonly shared information for occurrences of junction boxs. The set of shared information may include:

  • common properties with shared property sets
  • common representations
  • common materials
  • common composition of elements
  • common ports

It is used to define a junction box type specification indicating the specific product information that is common to all occurrences of that product type. The IfcJunctionBoxType may be declared within IfcProject or IfcProjectLibrary using IfcRelDeclares and may be exchanged with or without occurrences of the type. Occurrences of IfcJunctionBoxType are represented by instances of IfcJunctionBox. Refer to the documentation at IfcJunctionBox for supported property sets, materials, composition, and ports.
