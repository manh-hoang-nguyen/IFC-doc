The flow terminal type IfcStackTerminalType defines commonly shared information for occurrences of stack terminals. The set of shared information may include:

  • common properties with shared property sets
  • common representations
  • common materials
  • common composition of elements
  • common ports

It is used to define a stack terminal type specification indicating the specific product information that is common to all occurrences of that product type. The IfcStackTerminalType may be declared within IfcProject or IfcProjectLibrary using IfcRelDeclares and may be exchanged with or without occurrences of the type. Occurrences of IfcStackTerminalType are represented by instances of IfcStackTerminal. Refer to the documentation at IfcStackTerminal for supported property sets, materials, composition, and ports.
