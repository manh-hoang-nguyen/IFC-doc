The energy conversion device type IfcTubeBundleType defines commonly shared information for occurrences of tube bundles. The set of shared information may include:

  • common properties with shared property sets
  • common representations
  • common materials
  • common composition of elements
  • common ports

It is used to define a tube bundle type specification indicating the specific product information that is common to all occurrences of that product type. The IfcTubeBundleType may be declared within IfcProject or IfcProjectLibrary using IfcRelDeclares and may be exchanged with or without occurrences of the type. Occurrences of IfcTubeBundleType are represented by instances of IfcTubeBundle. Refer to the documentation at IfcTubeBundle for supported property sets, materials, composition, and ports.
